Explore Destinations
South India

TAMIL NADU

Famed for Dravidian-style temples, Tamil Nadu dazzles with Meenakshi Amman in Madurai, the sacred Ramanathaswamy Temple, and the ritual sunrises of Kanyakumari at India's southernmost tip.

God's Own Country

KERALA

Emerald backwaters, spice-scented hills, and pristine beaches make Kerala a dream. Houseboat rides on Alleppey and the lush valleys of Munnar await your soul.

Garden State

KARNATAKA

From the royal grandeur of Mysore Palace to the ancient ruins of Hampi and the coffee estates of Coorg, Karnataka blends history, nature and culture.

Queen of Hills

OOTY

Nestled in the Nilgiri hills, Ooty enchants with its toy train rides through tea estates, sprawling botanical gardens, and misty morning lake views.

Paradise on Earth

JAMMU & KASHMIR

Snow-capped peaks, shikara rides on Dal Lake, fragrant saffron fields — Jammu & Kashmir is the crown jewel of Indian landscapes.
